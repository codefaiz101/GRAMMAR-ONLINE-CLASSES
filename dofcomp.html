Degree of comparision

In simple words, the degree is the form of the adjective(Positive, comparative, and superlative) used to show different degrees or levels denoted by the adjective.

The three forms -positive, comparative, superlative used for the qualitative comparison of adjectives

Examples-

A)positive degree
  • He is a good player.
  • He is as good as his friend.
    • B)Comparative degree
  • He is better than his friend.

    • c)Superlative degree
  • He is the best player in his friends.
    • Types of the degree in English

    There are three main types of degrees. They are positive, comparative and superlative degrees.


    1)Positive degree

    Positive degree is used to show the similarity between two nouns or noun groups. It can be affirmative or negative.

    Its structure is as follows.

    As/so+ positive degree adjective+ as

    Example-

  • John is as brave as his brother.
  • John is not so/as brave as his brother.
    • 2)Comparative degree-

    The comparative degree is used to compare two nouns or noun groups. Its structure is as follows.
    Comparative degree adjective+ than.

    Example-

  • 1)John is braver than his brother.
  • John is not braver than his brother.
    • 3)Superlative degree-

    The superlative degree is used to show the highest position of the noun or noun group.


    Its structure is as follows.

    Example-

  • John is the bravest person in his family.
  • John is not the bravest person in his family.

    • How to change positive degree to comparative degree

  • Sam is not as kind as John.

    • Answer-John is kinder than Sam.

    Rules for changing positive to a comparative degree-
  • Begin the sentence with a noun higher in degree.
  • Use comparative degree adjectives.
    • How to change comparative degree sentences to positive degree sentences

    Examples-

  • I can play better than you.

    • Answer-You can not play as well as I.

    Rules for changing comparative to a positive degree.
  • First begin the sentence with the last noun or noun group.
  • Use or remove not as per the need to keep the meaning the same.
  • Use as+ positive degree adjective+ as
    • How to change superlative degree sentences to a positive and comparative degree

    Examples-

  • Education is the best option.

    • Positive degree-No other option is as good as education.
    Comparative degree-Education is better than any other option.

    Rules for changing superlative degree to positive and comparative degree.

    We can change the superlative degree to a positive degree by beginning the sentence with no other. Then use noun used after the adjective and use structure as+ positive degree adjective+ as
    For changing superlative degree to comparative degree, begin a sentence with the same noun used in superlative and then use Comparative degree adjective + than any other.
