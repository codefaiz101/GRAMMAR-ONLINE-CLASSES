Noun

Naming words are also known as nouns .nouns are names of persons ,places , things and animals

Common Noun

They are general names of the same kind of people ,places ,animals and things . they are written in small letters.
They begin with a capital letter only when they are at the beginning of a sentence.

Proper Noun

They are particular names of people, places , animals ,and things.

Catogry Common Noun Proper Noun
People Boys,aunt,sister,friends Rahul ,mr dixit, ruhi.
Places Park, school,city,country. Kanha National park, Podar International school, Mumbai,India.
Animals Dog, cat, fish, bird bruno, kitty
Things chair, room, pencil, book
Collective Noun

A collective noun denotes a group of individuals.

Examples: class (group of students), pride (group of lions), crew (group of sailors).

    • Abstract noun

    An abstract noun is the noun of a quality , action or status which we cannot see or touch. Abstract noun are ideas , concepts and feelings.

    Quality:

    kindness , goodness, honestly, wisdom, bravery.

    Action :

    laughter, hatred, movement, theft, judgement.

    State :

    childhood, boyhood, manhood, youth, slavery, freedom, sickness,poverty, death.

    The names of art and science (music, painting, chemistry, physics, etc.)are also abstract nouns

    Abstract noun are formed :
  • From adjective
    1. Goodness from good.
    2. Pleasure from please.
    3. Punishment from punish.

  • From verbs
    1. Obedience from obey.
    2. Pleasure from please.
    3. Kingdom from king.

  • From Common Nouns
    1. Childhood from child.
    2. Slavery from king.
    3. Kingdom from king.

    Since abstract nouns are uncountable , they have no plural from
